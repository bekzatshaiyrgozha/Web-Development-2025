Medeo

The Medeu is an outdoor speed skating rink located in the Medeu Valley, 1,691 meters above sea level. The rink is famous for its high-quality ice and picturesque surroundings.

Big Almaty Lake

Big Almaty Lake is an alpine reservoir, located 15 km from Almaty city center. The lake sits at 2,511 meters and is surrounded by breathtaking mountains.

Big Almaty Peak

Big Almaty Peak, at 3,680 meters, is a popular hiking destination offering stunning views of the Tien Shan range.
